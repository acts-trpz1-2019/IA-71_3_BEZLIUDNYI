The personal rewards of striving to be the best are even more meaningful in the development of young people than they are for automobiles. Instilling the values of leadership, goal-setting, discipline and teamwork in youth is an investment in everyone's future. It's why Mercedes-Benz USA is collaborating with the Laureus Sport for Good Foundation, with a commitment to train and place hundreds of coaches for post-school and in-school sports programs in communities around the country. Sports offer kids more than the opportunity to be physically active and have fun. It's also an opportunity to have a mentor who can guide them, help them learn how to deal with major life issues and help them grow stronger not just physically but in their character. As a Mercedes-Benz driver, you become a part of this effort to give hundreds of thousands of kids a better life today and all of us a brighter tomorrow.
Learn more about how Mercedes-Benz USA believes in giving back at MBUSA.com/community
